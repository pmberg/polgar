The POLGAR Project: Core

Core material is focused on basic technical content useful for all sorts of professionals, as well as core AI safety content useful for all different applications.

All nodes are considered to be MVP until further notice.

Academic Core

Calculus II

Advanced applications of (single-variable) derivatives and integrals, as well as sequences/series. Equivalent to the ending material of AP Calculus BC, or to MIT's 18.01A.

Prerequisites: Calculus I

Basics of probability and statistics: mean/median/mode, variance and standard deviation, inference, regression models, data literacy. Equivalent to an introductory college statistics course.
